Welcome !

I’m a passionate of technology with background in computer and electronic engineering. I worked in the semiconductors, high-tech and financial industries. This is my web page. You will find here some of the research I did in the past years as well as some information on past projects I got interested in.

Being curious and always eager to learn, I worked on very different topics during my life. I started doing research for EDA tools, then I applied such ideas to hardware architectures for system-on-chips (SoC) and embedded software. Eventually, I moved into the financial world and I enjoyed several years developing quantitative analysis and models for pricing/hedging and risk assessment of derivatives products. Then, I evolved toward tools and modeling for algorithmic trading. Of course, I got some interest in pricing models for patents and intangible assets. Recently, I was involved in IOT and machine learning, taking some fun with cybersecurity.

I told you, I’m curious so don’t blame me for being a tech vagabond!
